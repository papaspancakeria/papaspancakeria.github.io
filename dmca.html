1. Notification of Infringement

If you are a copyright owner or authorized to act on behalf of one, and you believe that your copyrighted work has been used in a way that constitutes copyright infringement, please provide a written DMCA notice containing the following:

  • A description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed.

  • The exact URL or location of the allegedly infringing material.

  • Your full name, mailing address, email address, and phone number.

  • A statement that you have a good-faith belief the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

  • A statement that the information in the notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, you are authorized to act on behalf of the owner of the exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.

  • Your physical or electronic signature.

Note: DMCA notices must be in English and must be submitted in writing.

2. Submission Method

Currently, we do not have a designated agent or email. If you believe content on this website violates your rights, please include your DMCA notice in a formal document and attempt to contact us through any available feedback mechanism on the Website.

3. Response and Removal

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice, we will:

  • Investigate the claim promptly.

  • Remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing content.

  • Notify the user responsible for the content (if applicable).

  • Provide a mechanism for a counter-notification, if necessary.

4. Repeat Infringers

Users who are found to repeatedly post infringing material may have their access permanently terminated.

5. False Claims

Under the DMCA, knowingly submitting false claims may subject you to liability for damages. Please ensure that your notice meets legal requirements and that you have a legitimate claim.
